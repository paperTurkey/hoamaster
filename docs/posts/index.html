Avalon News for March 2020

Avalon Reminders Help keep Avalon beautiful by maintaining your yard and home. Have your home power washed if there is mold on the exterior. Garbage, recycling, and yard trash should be put curbside only the night before pickup and receptacles removed by the day after pickup. Please take a bag to clean up after your dog when walking in Avalon. Lock vehicles left in the driveway and don’t leave anything valuable in sight.
